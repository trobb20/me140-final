Getting Started

  1. Download a circular Solidworks cam template by clicking the image.
  2. Edit the cam template to your liking, or if you are stuck, scroll further down for more ideas.
  3. Laser cut your cam.
  4. Use your lego kit and provided springs to design a mechanical system with rotational, translational, and plane motion.

Help with Laser Cutting

Laser Cutting Cam

To laser cut your cam, follow these steps:

  • Right click on the face of the cam
  • Select 'Export to DXF / DWG'
  • Save the DXF on your computer
  • Go laser cut your cam at the Nolop Makerspace

Hint:

Think like a builder:

  • How would you secure the cam with LEGO?
  • Can you find some LEGO pieces that would be able to "ride" on the cam?
  • How would you keep those LEGO pieces in contact with the cam?

Hint:

Think like a mathematictian:

  • How does a cam-rider move relative to a cam?
  • What does this mean mathematically?
  • How does an angular velocity relate to a tangential velocity?
